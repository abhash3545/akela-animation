Contractual Broiler Model

How our integration cycle works

Akela Poultry runs a structured broiler integration model with clear input support and performance-based grower settlement.

Step 1

Chick Placement

Day-old broiler chicks are supplied to registered partner growers as per cycle planning.

Step 2

Input Supply

Akela provides required feed, medicine, and essential poultry support items throughout the cycle.

Step 3

Technical Supervision

Field supervision and monitoring are done to maintain bird health, growth consistency, and FCR performance.

Step 4

Sale And Grower Settlement

After growth, birds are marketed by Akela and grower charges are paid based on performance, including FCR.
